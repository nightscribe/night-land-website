2025 October

October 14: In Which I Create this Journal

The point of starting entries now, before anyone else is likely to see them, is to design the journal layout. I think one page will cover about a quarter, depending on how many entries I make in that time period.

Today I created this Hour Slips Template page, and fixed some errors in the Essay Template.

2026 June

June 20: I Think I'll Start Updating the Hour Slips

This website is bigger than I remembered. I have all the Darkening stories copied over now except for Luna García y García's "Children of the Hive", which I need more art for. Some of the stories have placeholder art, because we never did have enough Darkening and Slow Sun era art in particular, and I don't want to reuse any art if I haven't contacted the artists or can't find out where the work came from. Our founding webmaster, the late Andy Robertson, licensed art and stories but didn't store the records in the website directory, except by accident. So I'll have to create or otherwise come by some more Darkening and Slow Sun illustrations.

Happily, I have more art for the time of the Last Redoubt.

I've finished moving Andy Robertson's novelette "Slope", and I'm illustrating it with Kawkawpa's images. Only one of them is evidently meant to be a direct illustration of "Slope" itself, but their menacing, unidentifiable startling contrasts and darkness suit the mood of the story admirably.

You won't be reading these posts till later, because I'm not ready to deploy this version of the site yet. But I think I'll start writing them anyway.
