Successor webmaster Kate Coady did not find notes as to where the original webmaster, the late Andy Robertson, came by the other images in this sequence. Andy Robertson had many contacts, including artists, from his time at Interzone. She thinks the Red Sun image is a representation of Barnard's Star, probably from a scientific journal in the early 1970s. If you know who created the other images, please write to the current webmaster.
