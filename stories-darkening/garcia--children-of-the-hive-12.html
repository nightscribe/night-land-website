. . . suddenly.

As he came back to consciousness, several things struck his senses simultaneously, and he didn't know which had woken him. The wound in his back hurt terribly. His level of energy had never been so low. And the vacuum resounded with the voices of his four brothers.

"Come", cried out the radio waves through the distance. "Come to the light."

He called back. "Where are you, brothers?"

They transmitted their positions in relation to each other. His brain used that knowledge and the slight asyncronicity in their voices to calculate instinctively his exact position in relation to them.

They were almost on the other side of the Valley. Far away.
