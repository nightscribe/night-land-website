Perhaps the Hive was a kind of forest. Her appearance was mineral, of course, but her mobility reminded him of forests. Or silent coral. Perhaps the Hive was intelligent coral.
 
Perhaps that was why she was afraid of dying. Perhaps that was why she could forget her own children and attack them like enemies. She was carbon-based instead of silicon-based. That could be important.
 
He promised himself he would inform his brothers, when they were near enough and he was recovered.
 
Soon. Yes, soon. Meanwhile, he let himself fall in a refreshing sleep.
 
While he dreampt . . .
