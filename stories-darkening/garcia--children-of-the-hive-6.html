Then, the Hive had been something different, something with numerous little wheels that moved chasing the Sun. And before still, the Sun had moved so fast that the Hive hadn't been able to follow it. The five giants would have perceived the Sun only as a bright line on the sky, so fast it moved, and the whole sky would have seemed to flicker constantly to them between the absence and presence of light.
