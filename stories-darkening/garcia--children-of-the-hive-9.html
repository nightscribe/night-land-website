. . . and the last to leave. After taking a last glance to the trail that ended in the already distant Hive, he started to walk towards the South. And he had hardly covered a few steps when he felt an excruciating pain in his back.

He ran desperately. He was hounded by the buzzing of the Hive, that buzzing in some ways similar to the forest buzzing, but much more intense.

What if . . .?
